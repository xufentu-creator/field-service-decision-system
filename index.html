Field Service Decision System｜现场服务决策系统

An applied research prototype exploring how field service requests can be structured into clear decision pathways while keeping human judgment central.
一个应用研究原型，用来探索现场服务请求如何被结构化为清晰的决策路径，同时保留人类判断的核心位置。

This prototype does not replace human judgment. It organizes service information, clarifies possible options, and supports human-confirmed decisions.
这个原型不取代人类判断。它用于整理服务信息、明确可能方案，并支持由人确认的决策过程。

Research focus:
Human Judgment · Decision Architecture · Complex Systems · Service Request Structuring · Human-Confirmed Decisions

Research foundation: Human Judgment as Structural Constraint
Author: Xufen Tu
